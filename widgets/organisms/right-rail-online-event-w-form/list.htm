Arm yourself with the tools to make intelligent, data-driven decisions for VM optimization.

Optimization for VMware can be tricky. But with the right tools, finding the balance for your environment can be much easier.

Optimize your VMware implementation to avoid under- or over-utilization — and boost performance and efficiency. Join this webcast to learn about Dell tools that can help you make intelligent, data-driven decisions for VM optimization throughout your entire virtual environment. Use intelligent analytics and expert advice to tune and optimize your environment using historical usage data and take corrective action immediately or via automation.

What you will learn

At the end of this webcast, you'll know how the right tools can help you:

  • See all over- and under-utilized virtual machines.
  • Take corrective action or define automation.
  • Clean up waste to lower operating expenses.
