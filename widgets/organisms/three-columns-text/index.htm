Challange

GoPro needed to find a way to quickly connect NetSuite with value-added networks (VANs) and AS2 connections in order to meet the strict EDI compliance standards of key retailers who sell GoPro cameras.

Solution

Deploy Dell Boomi to integrate NetSuite to VANs & AS2 Connections and to integrate NetSuite to 3rd party logistics. Engaged with Boomi services to speed deployment.

Benefits

  • 25 to 30% of transaction flow through EDI
  • Significantly improved operational efficiencies
  • 300% year-over-year growth that can scale to meet shifts in demand
